    CHOCOLATE COOKIES
    1 ½ cups butter, room temp.
    1 cup sugar
    1 large egg
    ½ tbsp. vanilla extract
    ⅜ cup cocoa powder
    1 cup all-purpose flour
    ½ tsp. baking soda
    ¼ tsp. salt
    white sugar for garnish (optional)


    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar. Mix eggs and vanilla extract into previous mixture until combined.

    2. In a medium bowl, mix the cocoa powder, flour, baking soda, and salt. Slowly add the dry ingredients to wet and continue mixing until incorporated.

    3. Roll the dough into 2 logs that are about 2-inches high and 1-foot long. Wrap them in waxed paper and place in the refrigerator for 2 hours.

    4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

    5. Once thoroughly chilled, slice the cookies into 1/2-inch thick rounds and cover with sanding sugar. Place on a parchment paper lined cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes.

    6. Remove and place onto a wire rack to cool. Enjoy!
