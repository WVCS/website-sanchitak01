    SNICKERDOODLE COOKIES
    2 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
    1 ½ cups sugar
    1 cup softened butter (2 sticks)
    2 large eggs
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    1 ½ teaspoons cream of tartar
    1 teaspoon salt
    ½ teaspoon baking soda


    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar together for 4-5 minutes until light and fluffy. Scrape the sides of the bowl and then add the eggs and vanilla. Cream for 1-2 more minutes.

    2. Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt until just combined.

    3. Wrap dough and refrigerate for 20-30 minutes. In a small dish, combine ¼ cup sugar and 1 ½ tbsp cinnamon. Take out dough and roll into small balls until round and smooth. Coat in sugar-cinnamon mixture.

    4. Place on a parchment-lined tray and flatten slightly to keep from puffing up in the middle.

    5. Bake for about 10 minutes, or until cookies are golden-brown. Let cool on baking tray, then cool completely on cooling rack.
