    BUTTER COOKIES (BENNE BISCUITS)
    1 cup butter (225 grams)
    ½ powdered sugar/icing sugar (75 grams)
    1 ½ cups maida/ all-purpose flour (250 grams)
    pinch of salt


    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Whisk softened butter for a minute until smooth

    2. Add all of sugar and continue to whisk until fluffy and smooth

    3. Mix in sifted flour and salt, and continue to mix dough without overmixing.

    4. Roll a small ball of dough, and place in a tray. Then stab a fork to create design.

    5. Preheat oven to 180° C, or 356° F for ten minutes.

    6. Cool in tray and store in airtight container.
