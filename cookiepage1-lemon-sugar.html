    LEMON SUGAR COOKIES
    1 cup butter softened
    1 3/4 cup sugar
    1 egg
    1 teaspoon vanilla
    juice of 1 lemon *see notes
    1 teaspoon baking soda
    1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    3 cups flour


    INSTRUCTIONS

    1. Cream butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla and mix well.

    2. Add lemon juice, salt, soda, and baking powder and mix well.

    3. Add flour and mix until combined. Dough should be thick and crumbly.

    4. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes.


    Note:
    *This cookie has a VERY mild lemon flavor. Do NOT add extra lemon juice, or it will affect the texture of the cookie. For a stronger lemon flavor, add the zest of one lemon, or Â½ teaspoon of lemon extract. You can also add a simple glaze made with lemon juice and powdered sugar.
